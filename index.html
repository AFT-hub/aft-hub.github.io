File Naming Convention

Textbooks

Example:
Ch 2_Fresh Fruit Broken Bodies_a8e_RD.docx
Details:
[Chapter number]_[Title of work]_[a8e]_[Your Intials]

Journal article/ Readings

Example:
Berzin_Meeting Social Behavior Health_a8e_CB.docx
Details:
[Author]_[Title of work]_[a8e]_[Your Intials]

First Page Items

Textbooks

While formatting textbooks, please include the book title in the begining of the file as below.
Example:

Fresh Fruit, Broken Bodies


Seth Holmes
Page 1

Chapter 2: Introduction: Worth Risking your life?


...
Details:
Textbook Title: [heading 1] Fresh Fruit, Broken Bodies [/heading 1]
Seth Holmes
Page Number: [Strong] Page 10 [/Strong]
Chapter Title [heading 1] Chapter 2: Introduction: Worth Risking your life? [/heading 1] ...

Journal article/ Readings

Example:

Journal of School Health

American School Health Association
Page 1

Meeting the Social and Behavioral Health Needs of Students: Rethinking the Relationship between Teachers and School Social Workers

Stephanie Cosner Berzin, PhD, MSSW, Kimberly H. McManama O'Brien, LICSW, Any Frey, PhD, Michael S Kelly, PhD ...
Details:
Journal Title (if available): [heading 1] Journal of School Health [/heading 1]
American School Health Association
Page Number: [Strong] Page 1 [/Strong]
Article Title: [heading 1] Meeting the Social and Behavioral Health Needs of Students: Rethinking the Relationship between Teachers and School Social Workers [/heading 1]
...
