File Naming Convention

All files should follow the following naming convention
Example:
Ch 2_Fresh Fruit Broken Bodies_a8e_RD.docx
Details: [Chapter number]_[Title of work]_[a8e]_[Your Intials]

First Page Items

Textbooks

While formatting textbooks, please include the book title in the begining of the file as below.
Example:

Fresh Fruit, Broken Bodies


Seth Holmes
Page 1

Chapter 2: Introduction: Worth Risking your life?


...
Details:
[heading 1] Fresh Fruit, Broken Bodies [/heading 1] Seth Holmes [Strong] Page 1 [/Strong] [heading 1] Chapter 2: Introduction: Worth Risking your life? [/heading 1] ...
